Other Routes
and Highways

There are some other routes and highways at the final administrative level of the American Highway system including county highways, forest highways, and bicycle routes. The table down below takes you on a road trip down all the different types of other routes and highways we have in America besides Interstate highways, U.S. highways, and state routes.
